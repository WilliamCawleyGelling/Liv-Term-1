Game of Dice

Instructions

Welcome to the Game of Dice. Start by choosing how many dice you wish to play with, from 3 to 6. Press the START button to begin. Your dice will then automatically roll and the first round will play. Your score will then be calculated according to the rules below.

  • All the same: You get 60 points for having all the dice the same, plus the sum of all the dice
  • All but one the same: You get 40 points for having all but one the same, plus the sum of all the dice
  • A run: You get 20 points for having a run of dice for exanple (2,3,4,5). Plus the sum of all the dice
  • No repeats: You get the sum of the dice for having dice with all diffrent values that are not a run
  • None of the above: If none of the above conditions are met then you get 0 for the round.

You will then have the option to roll again or to finish. On finishing your total score will be displayed as well as the number of rounds played and your avarage score. You can play again by pressing the play again button.
